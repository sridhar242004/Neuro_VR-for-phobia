Frequently Asked Questions

How does NeuroVR Therapy work?

NeuroVR Therapy combines virtual reality technology with evidence-based psychological techniques to create immersive environments where you can safely confront and overcome your phobias. The therapy is personalized and adapts to your progress in real-time.

Is NeuroVR Therapy safe?

Yes, NeuroVR Therapy is completely safe. The virtual environments are carefully designed and controlled, allowing you to face your fears gradually and at your own pace. Our team of expert psychologists oversees the entire process to ensure your well-being.
